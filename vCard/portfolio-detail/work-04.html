LASR OS

About Project

LASR is a mobile operating system concept with 15 core apps. Made with design principles and focused on sincerity, personalization and efficiency.

Beyond a standalone app or service, design systems can cover a wide array of products. Design systems enable us to build better products faster by making design reusableâ€”reusability makes scale possible. They also reduce overall design/technical debt. We wanted to explore designing for systems, with the framework of designing a mobile OS. We used design principles to unite our overall design.

Create a design language and a set of shared design principles to guide the system design. Design the entire mobile OS using the design language, and a full collection of core apps.
