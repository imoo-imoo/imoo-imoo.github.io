Cona Messenger

About Project

This is a concept of a messenger for the iPhone X (iOS platform). On the screens you can see a list with your chats, conversation window and a profile of your friend in a popup view. You can quickly go to settings from the pop up view as well as check media files, share this contact with others, and block the user.

Clear and easy way to stay in touch with your close ones!


Client : UNZIZNU / Year : 2017
