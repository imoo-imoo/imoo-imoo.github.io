LASR-2 OS

About Project

LASR is a mobile operating system concept with 15 core apps. Made with design principles and focused on sincerity, personalization and efficiency. This project is my reimagination of one of the greatest piece of software ever. It is a mixture of fundamentals that came to life in 2010 and today's standards in graphic and interface design.
