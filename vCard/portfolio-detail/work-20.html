Pique-Nique Coffee

About Project

Pique-Nique Coffee is a minimal cafe shop. They offer coffee and also healthy, nutritious and delicious dishes to their customers. I were tasked to create a brand new logo that will set them apart from the local competition. With our research we decided to go with a black and white concept keeping it simple and clean.


Client : Pique-Nique Coffee / Year : 2015
