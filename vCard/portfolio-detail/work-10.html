Mobile Office Assistant

About Project

Mobile Office Assistant (Mobile OA) is an product for employees in company, It integrated check-in system, business news feed, business contacts, work tasks, masssnger, documents manager and meeting online (Video chat) service.

As the product director, I was responsible for the full design of Mobile OS. I worked out the product from start to finish, working with developers to make Mobile OS a reality.
