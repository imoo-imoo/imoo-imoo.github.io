RIBBON

About Project

Ribbon is a new interpretation of a newspaper app for iPhone with new style design, user friendly UX and extremely interesting idea. Create your own articles or add favourite articles to your newsfeed, find new information, read the hottest blogs, share youe expertise and have enjoy this.

Ribbon will change this and collect of each different news platform out there the articles and give it to you. You personalize your app with all the topics you are interessted like sports, politics, science and more. Also you specific these topics.


Client : Ribbon / Year : 2014
