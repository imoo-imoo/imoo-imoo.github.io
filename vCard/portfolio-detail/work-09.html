CheerWrap

About Project

It's a project about help CheerWrap team redesign the mobile app, improve user experience, provide a better app experience, launch some new features and more user-friendly.

Singapore's Mobile Gift Card Mall! CheerWrap is gifting made simple. You can send e-gift cards from awesome brands, when you want, wherever you are, in just seconds! It's no longer just about the special occasions. Whether it's for birthdays, the holiday seasons, rewarding someone for a job well done, or just because you want to spread cheer...we've got you covered. When you give the gift of CheerWrap, your recipients can choose exactly what they want so you know it's always going to be the right gift.
