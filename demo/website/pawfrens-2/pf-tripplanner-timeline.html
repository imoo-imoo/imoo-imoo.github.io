01

Microchip Your Pet

Complete by Aug 26, 2025

A valid microchip is the foundation of your pet’s travel documentation. It helps verify your pet’s identity throughout the journey and ensures that vaccination records and health certificates are properly linked. For many destinations, the microchip must be implanted before or on the same day as the rabies vaccination for the record to be accepted.

02

Get Rabies Vaccination

Due Jul 21, 2021 – Aug 26, 2025

Once your pet is microchipped, a valid rabies vaccination is usually one of the most important travel requirements. The vaccine must meet the destination’s timing and validity rules, and the documentation must match your pet’s registered microchip details. Keeping the vaccination certificate safe is essential, as it will be needed during later travel steps.

03

Complete Health Check

Schedule between Mar 29 – Apr 28

BAs your departure date approaches, your pet may need an official veterinary examination to confirm they are healthy and ready for travel. This visit may include reviewing your pet’s microchip, vaccine records, and any additional destination-specific requirements. Because this step often has a strict timing window, planning ahead is key.

04

You're Ready to Travel

Ready by Apr 28, 2026

Once all required steps have been completed, your pet is officially prepared for the journey. With the necessary identification, vaccinations, and health documentation in place, you can move forward knowing you’ve met the required travel conditions for your destination.

Pet Travel Doesn’t Have to Be Complicated

International pet travel often involves multiple documents, medical requirements, and strict deadlines that can be difficult to keep track of. Pawfrens turns that complexity into a simple, guided experience — helping you understand the process clearly, stay organized, and prepare each step with confidence.

Health Certificates & Vet Visits Made Simple

From vaccination records to official health certificates, veterinary requirements are one of the most important parts of traveling with a pet. Pawfrens helps you understand what’s needed, when to schedule appointments, and how to prepare the right records before departure.

Country-Specific Rules, Clearly Explained

Every destination has its own pet entry rules, and missing even one detail can create unnecessary delays. Pawfrens organizes those country-specific requirements into a clear, structured checklist so you can prepare accurately without digging through confusing regulations.

Free to Use, No Account Needed

Getting started should be simple. With Pawfrens, you can instantly generate a personalized travel checklist without creating an account, making it easy to plan, review, download, and share your pet’s travel requirements whenever you need them.
