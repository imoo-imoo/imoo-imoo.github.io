What is DeMIN?

The Decentralized Mobile Infrastructure Network (DeMIN) harnesses the collective power of individual smartphones and devices. Unlike traditional centralized infrastructure, DeMIN distributes the operations across millions of devices, each acting as a lite node. This enhances the network‚Äôs resilience, scalability, and security, while also putting control back into the hands of the users.

With 4.88 billion users worldwide, accounting for 60.42% of the global population, this initiative aims to democratize access to smartphones ensuring that technology serves everyone, not just the privileged few.
