FAQs

How much does a product strategy project cost?
Every project is different. The scope depends on business goals, research requirements, stakeholder involvement and expected deliverables. We usually begin with a discovery session before recommending the most suitable approach.
Do I need a fully developed idea before contacting N3Point?
Not at all. Many clients come to us with early-stage concepts, business challenges or market opportunities. Our role is to help shape those ideas into clear product strategies.
What happens after the strategy phase?
N3Point supports the entire product journey. From strategy and product planning to UX/UI design, development, branding and long-term growth initiatives.
Do you work with startups or large enterprises?
Both. Our experience spans early-stage startups, scale-ups and enterprise organisations across multiple industries and markets.
Can N3Point support international projects?
Yes. Our team has delivered products and digital experiences across Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam, Indonesia, Europe and beyond.
