Up Mobile reimagined what a Web3 device could become by bringing blockchain, AI and everyday mobile experiences into a single ecosystem. Working alongside the Up team, N3Point contributed across product strategy, brand identity, engineering collaboration and launch execution, helping turn an ambitious vision into a real product.

Our
Approach

We approached the project as one continuous product instead of a collection of separate deliverables. Branding, engineering, industrial design and digital experiences evolved together around the same product vision, allowing every touchpoint to feel connected from the very beginning.

  • Think in systems, not screens.
  • Design and engineering evolve together.
  • Keep every touchpoint visually connected.
  • Build for launch, not presentation.
  • Reduce complexity wherever possible.
