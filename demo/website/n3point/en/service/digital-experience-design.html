FAQs

What does Digital Experience Design include?
Digital Experience Design goes beyond UI and UX. It includes digital products, websites, mobile applications, brand experiences, design systems, visual identities and customer-facing touchpoints. Our goal is to create experiences that feel consistent, memorable and aligned with your business.
Can N3Point help with branding and visual identity?
Yes. From logo design and brand guidelines to marketing materials and motion graphics, we help organisations build strong and consistent brands across digital and physical channels.
Do you only design, or can you also build?
We do both. N3Point combines strategy, design and technology, allowing us to support projects from concept through development and launch.
Can you work with our existing team?
Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with internal teams, external agencies and technology partners, adapting our process to fit each organisation.
What industries do you work with?
Our experience spans retail, aviation, real estate, finance, technology, entertainment and customer loyalty ecosystems. We focus on solving business challenges, rather than limiting ourselves to a specific industry.
Do you work internationally?
Yes. Our team has delivered projects across Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Vietnam, Australia, Europe and beyond, working with organisations of various sizes and stages.
