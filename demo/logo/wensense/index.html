Logo Design Concept – WenSense

WenSense is a contemporary Oriental fragrance brand rooted in the quiet balance between atmosphere, ritual, and modern living. Inspired by Asian incense culture and contemporary spatial aesthetics, the identity explores how scent can become part of emotional architecture shaping stillness, memory, and presence through design.

Geometry & Symbolism: The logomark reconstructs the Chinese characters 「雯香」into a modular geometric symbol, balancing structure, rhythm, and abstraction through carefully reduced forms.

Atmosphere & Stillness: Soft visual spacing and restrained compositions reflect the emotional calmness associated with incense rituals, spatial silence, and contemplative living.

Not everything meaningful needs to be loud. WenSense was designed as a quiet expression of atmosphere, ritual, and contemporary Oriental identity.
