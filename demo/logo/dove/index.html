Dove - Logo Concept.

A collection of logo concept for "Dove" a girl rapper in "The Rap of China" and record label.

  • Client
    Dove Record Label
  • Technologies
    Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects
  • Type
    Logomark, Logotype
  • Website
    none
  • Year
    2024

I envisioned some classic, yet edgy design, that clearly communicates the brand’s character. Also I came up with some fusion of bold and cute logos that features a record and sword, accompanied by a legible futuristic and technological fonts. The daring futuristic style script was chosen because it was a typographical trend in the new age of hip hop.

Cutting and pasting is the essence of what hip-hop culture is all about for me. It’s about drawing from what’s around you, and subverting it and decontextualizing it.

Richard Lee.
Freelancer - UNZIZNU
